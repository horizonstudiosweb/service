Our projects

Three studio brands under one vision.

Horizon Resort

Horizon Resort is a modern Roblox resort experience designed to create a relaxed and realistic vacation atmosphere. The project focuses on modern rooms, star-level comfort, spa areas, exclusive lounges and a premium resort feeling in cooperation with AVIA Assistance.

Horizon Hotels

Horizon Hotels is planned as a modern BNB-inspired hotel experience with a clean four-star hotel style. The concept includes simple modern rooms, an indoor pool, a spa area, shops, breakfast areas and restaurant spaces for a complete hotel stay.

GREDDO. germany

GREDDO germany is a fast-food brand project and currently operates as one main location. Players can enjoy burgers, sweet snacks and a tasty modern food-service atmosphere with a clean and recognizable brand vibe.
